Who are we?

We are a search platform that aims to create a new food-tasting experience by providing a varied list of dishes from all over the world based on criterias such as ingredients and country of origin. If you're looking to try something new, you can search for a meal either randomly or by specific criterias and each result will give the ingredient list and instructions.
